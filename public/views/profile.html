My Story

When I was a kid, I always wanted to be an Artist, my father discovered my interest and bought me some colors and a drawing book, I soon began painting, few years passed by and my father was worried like every other Indian dad thinking that I'd go ahead and pursue a career in Artistry of Color, he pursued me and put me into studying Mathematics, I excelled in my class and after a few years, during my undergraduate career, my childhood instincts kicked in and I told my dad " you can stop me from putting color on the paper but you can't take me away from painting the web beautiful " and that is how it all began.
